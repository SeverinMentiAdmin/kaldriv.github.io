ABOUT

Senior expertise, lean delivery

Kaldriv OÜ is a boutique banking technology consultancy headquartered in Tallinn, Estonia, serving clients across the DACH region and the Baltic states. Founded by a Severin Menti with over a decade of Temenos practice leadership across Asia, Europe and the Baltics, the firm brings principal-level expertise to every engagement — End - to - End.

Our background includes large-scale Temenos implementations at Tier 1 banks, Basel III regulatory implementations, cross-border banking migrations, and private wealth platform architecture. We operate as an independent OÜ registered in Estonia, giving clients a streamlined EU-based contracting structure with full GDPR compliance.
